Bacon, Lettuce & Tomato sandwich

5 MINUTE RECIPE

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices crisp bacon
  • 2 leaves lettuce
  • 2 slices of tomato
  • 2 tbsp. mayonnaise
  • 2 slices of whole wheat bread, toasted

Method:

  1. Spread both slices of lightly toasted sandwich bread with mayonnaise on one side only.
  2. Add several leaves of lettuce on one piece of bread.
  3. Add several slices of thickly sliced garden tomato on top.
  4. Layer on 2-3 slices of crisp bacon over the tomatoes.
  5. Top with second slice of lightly toasted sandwich bread.
  6. Gently slice in half with serrated bread knife, being careful not to smush your sandwich.
  7. Serve immediately with a dill pickle!
